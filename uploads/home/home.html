Institute for Muslim Affairs
in Myanmar

We are an independent and non-governmental scholarly think tank
to promote the issues of Muslim minority rights, highlighting
minority participation in the political process of Myanmar with
unique strategies and initiatives. We will follow liberal democratic values
and foster the development of multiculturalism in the
diverse communities of Burma.
